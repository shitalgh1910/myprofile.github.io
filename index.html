Potatoes Disease Classification and Recognition

In this project, I Developed a machine learning model to classify potato plant diseases (early blight, late blight, and healthy) to aid in early detection and treatment. I Implemented Convolutional Neural Network (CNN), Support Vector Machine (SVM), and k-Nearest Neighbours (KNN) algorithms. I Trained CNN, SVM, and KNN models on training, testing and validation dataset, achieving highest accuracy with CNN at 98.05%. Out of three algorithms, CNN model demonstrated superior performance in disease classification with a validation accuracy of 98%.
